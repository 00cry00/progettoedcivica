THE FREEDOM TO COMMUNICATE (Art. 15 - Art. 16)

Article 15: Freedom and secrecy of correspondence

  • Text: "The freedom and secrecy of correspondence and of every other form of communication are inviolable. Their limitation can only occur by a reasoned act of the judicial authority, with the guarantees established by the law."
  • Meaning: This article guarantees that every citizen can communicate freely (through letters, phone calls, emails, etc.) without interference or arbitrary control by the state or third parties.

Article 16: Freedom of movement and residence

  • Text: "Every citizen may move and reside freely in any part of the national territory, except for limitations established by law for reasons of health or security. No restriction can be imposed for political reasons."
  • Meaning: This article recognizes the right to move freely within the national territory and to choose where to reside.

Article 6: Protection of linguistic minorities

  • Text: "The Republic shall protect the linguistic minorities present within its borders."
  • Meaning: This article guarantees the preservation and promotion of the linguistic heritage of Italy. It recognizes the importance of safeguarding languages other than Italian, which are part of the cultural identity of various regions of the country.
